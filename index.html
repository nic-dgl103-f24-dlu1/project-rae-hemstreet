Plan Your Escape to the Gardens

Where Passion for Nature Blossoms

Kitty Coleman Woodland Gardens is a labor of love, born from one man's vision to create a place of beauty and serenity for all to enjoy.

Nestled in Courtenay, BC, the gardens feature one of Western Canada's largest rhododendron collections with over 3,000 plants. The 24-acre woodland sanctuary is also home to native plants, birds, and wildflowers, with winding paths and serene water features. Recognized as one of the world's finest informal show gardens, it offers a peaceful retreat for nature lovers.

Step Into the Garden: Upcoming Events and Activities

We offer a range of events throughout the year, from art shows and the spring Rhododendron Festival to summer tours and workshops. Our festive Christmas programs add seasonal charm, making the gardens a year-round destination for nature lovers and visitors of all ages.
