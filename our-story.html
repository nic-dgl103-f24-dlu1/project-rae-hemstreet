Our Story, Rooted in Nature

From Wilderness to Wonder

Nestled in the heart of the Comox Valley, Kitty Coleman Woodland Gardens is a labor of love brought to life by Bryan Zimmerman's vision and dedication. Known locally for his Christmas Tree Farm and Santa's Barn, Bryan saw beyond the winter magic of his property. As he began clearing the dense brush on his 24-acre forested land, he uncovered a hidden gem—an enchanting forest floor waiting to be shared.

Inspired by the natural beauty and unique topography of the land, Bryan envisioned a woodland sanctuary where visitors could connect with nature. Over two years, he worked tirelessly to bring his dream to life. With just a wheelbarrow, a strong back, and an unwavering commitment to preserving the environment, Bryan cleared the underbrush by hand, ensuring minimal disruption to the land's natural ecosystem.

The result is a breathtaking network of over a mile of bark mulch pathways that wind through the woods, inviting exploration and wonder. Each step reflects Bryan's care and respect for nature, creating a serene escape where the beauty of the forest speaks for itself.
